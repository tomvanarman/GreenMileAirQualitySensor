# Product vision

## Product Name:

- Climate Measuring Box (CMB)

## Vision Statement:

- Inform citizens about the quality of life in the area by displaying the current quality of life
  with the device and informing them about quality of life over time through data visualisation

## Target Group

- Urban planners
- City policymakers to make more informed decicions.
- People on the street so they can see the quality of life for the day.

## Needs

- Real-time visibility into quality of life and other measurements.
- Historical reports about sensor readings to see the green mile progression.
- Intergration with Grafana.
- Easily scalable.
- Water resistant
- Easily changeable batteries
- Power efficiency

## Product

- Embedded Component: Sensor box with LTE connection that report back to a database.
- Back-end Component: Database with Grafana instance to show analytical data in graphs.

## Value

- For Policy makers: Make more informed decicions
- For urban planners: Lower emmision rates and smarter decicion making
- For the people outside: Having a better idea about the quality of life outside and bringing it to
  attention.

## Business Goals

- Monitor progression of the green mile over time.
- Inform the public and policy makers about the quality of life
- Build a scalable platform for future expansion.
